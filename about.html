Northwood Outreach

Ours is a young and growing country church, established in 1983. We serve families in Esko, Cloquet, Carlton and other surrounding communities.

Since the completion of our church building, Northwood has put forth a concerted effort toward outreach. Locally, recent outreach efforts have included Blanket Buddies (Fleece yard goods are collected and made into blankets, hats, mittens and scarfs and given to those in need.) and contributions to the Minnesota March Food Drive through the Carlton County Food Shelf. Nationally, a group of youth and adults from Northwood went to New Orleans in June of 2009 on a mission trip and we are currently planning for a mission trip to the Rio Bravo Mission in December of 2015 with Crossroads Church. Globally, we have participated in Operation Christmas Shoebox, collected boxes for soldiers, and sent money to hurricane victims.

Monthly collections for the Carleton County Food Shelf unless otherwise noted.

Northwood Mission

Spread the Good News of Jesus Christ and follow His example through fostering fellowship and acceptance, reaching to those in need, and engaging in social justice and stewardship.

Northwood Vision

A small church, with a big heart.

An open, welcoming, seven-day-a-week church that shares God's spirit, serves as a community hub and sponsors good works.
