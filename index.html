A small church with a big heart

We are a small church looking to expand in our mission and vision. We welcome everyone who walks through our doors in search of a relationship with Jesus Christ. We currently provide a Sunday morning worship service at 9 AM, a time of fellowship after the service and pre-school thru adult Sunday school from 10:30 - 11:30. We provide monthly fellowship opportunities and services to the surrounding communities of Esko, Cloquet, Carlton and Duluth. We are looking to broaden and deepen our individual relationships with Christ through a variety of special seasonal worhip opportunites. Ours is an open communion table and everyone is welcome to take communion which we offer on the first Sunday (usually) of the month. YOU ARE INVITED TO JOIN US - THERE IS A PLACE FOR YOU!

Find us on Sunday

Please find us at 62 West Highway 61 Esko, Minnesota. Esko is about halfway between Duluth and Cloquet, Minnesota and can be reached from I-35, taking the exit at mile post marker #242 and heading north onto Thompson Rd. Turn west at the town's center onto Hwy 61. Northwood Church is on the south side of the divided highway about 1 mile west of the intersection of Thompson Rd and Hwy 61.
